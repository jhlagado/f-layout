### Layout Containers

Layout occurs in either rows or columns. Elements placed in a container will be layed out according to the rules of that container.

### Laying out a rows

```html
<div class="f-layout f-row">
  <span>1</span>
  <span>2</span>
  <span>3</span>
</div>
```
